Welcome to BBCR-INR

The lab is dedicated to advancing the general field of information science, network theory, artificial intelligence, information security, carring out research on basic theories and key technologies for intelligent 6G networks, and designing scalable, highly reliable, and highly secure intelligent networks.

Get Started

Research Interests

Our mission is to conduct impactful research in new broadband communication networks, space-air-ground integrated networks, industrial internet, vehicular networks and others, and play a key role in intelligent manufacturing, smart cities and other important fields, supporting technological innovation in corresponding vertical industries.

Ph.D Student Program

For details, please click here
  • Application method: The program admits a "application-assessment" system.
  • Mode of study: The basic study period is 4 years. The course study is carried out in Lianpei University, and the thesis research is carried out in Pengcheng Laboratory Research topics are selected from major national scientific research tasks.
  • Research direction: for 6G network, develop basic theories and key technologies such as network virtualization, network intelligence, and network security.
  • Mentoring: Each doctoral student is equipped with a joint steering group composed of university supervisors, laboratory supervisors and enterprise supervisors.
